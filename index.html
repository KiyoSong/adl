ADL is a global organization that focuses on supporting and promoting young filmmakers and their creations. It aims to offer an international platform for young film creators to engage in cross-cultural cooperation.
Through various activities and projects, ADL encourages young filmmakers to explore new realms of cinematic art, driving innovation and diversity in the film industry, and promoting cultural exchange among nations.
